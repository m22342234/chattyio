About Chattyio

Real conversations with real people — no account, no history, no noise.

What is Chattyio?

Chattyio is a free anonymous text chat platform. You join a live lobby, browse users from around the world, and start a conversation in seconds. When the session ends, the conversation is gone — no logs, no history, no trace.

There's no profile to build, no followers to accumulate, and no algorithm deciding what you see. Just a room full of people and the option to talk to any of them.

How it works

1

Pick a name

Choose a display name, your age, gender, and country. No email, no password.

2

Browse the lobby

See who's online. Users are grouped by country. Search by name or hit Random Match.

3

Start chatting

Click any user to open a chat. Messages are delivered instantly. Nothing is saved.

Privacy by design

We don't store messages. We don't keep conversation logs. Your chat history exists only in your browser's memory for the duration of your session — the moment you close the tab, it's gone. Our server acts as a relay only; it forwards messages and immediately discards them.

  • No account or email required
  • Messages are never written to disk or a database
  • IP addresses are held in memory only and cleared on disconnect
  • No tracking cookies or analytics scripts

Who it's for

Chattyio is for adults (18+) who want to have genuine conversations without creating an account or leaving a permanent digital footprint. Whether you want to meet people from other countries, practice a language, or just talk to someone new — the lobby is open.

Safety first

Anonymity doesn't mean anything goes. Chattyio has zero tolerance for CSAM, exploitation, harassment, or illegal content. Every user can report a violator with one click — reported users are immediately removed and their IP is blocked.

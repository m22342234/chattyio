What is anonymous chat?

Anonymous chat lets you talk to other people without revealing your real identity. You don't need to create an account, verify an email address, or connect a phone number. You pick a nickname and start talking.

People use anonymous chat for all kinds of reasons: meeting people from other countries, practicing a foreign language with a native speaker, having an honest conversation without social consequences, or simply talking to someone new when their usual circle isn't available.

The key is that the conversation doesn't follow you. When you close the tab, it's gone.

How Chattyio achieves real anonymity

No account, no identity

No email, phone number, or password. You choose a display name — that's the only thing other users ever see.

Zero message retention

Messages are relayed between users in real time and immediately discarded. Nothing is written to a database or log file — ever.

IP deleted on disconnect

Your IP address is held in server memory only while you are connected — for rate limiting and abuse prevention. It is deleted the instant you disconnect.

No tracking, no cookies

Chattyio sets no tracking cookies and runs no analytics that identify individual users. What you do here stays here.

Frequently asked questions

Is Chattyio truly anonymous?

Yes. No email, phone, or account required. Your IP is held in memory only while connected and deleted on disconnect. Messages are never stored. The only identifying information anyone sees is the display name you choose.

Can someone trace me through Chattyio?

Chattyio doesn't store messages, build profiles, or log IPs after a session ends. The only way someone could identify you is if you share personal information in the chat yourself.

Do I need to download anything?

No. Chattyio runs in any modern browser. No app, no plugin, no extension.

What happens to my messages when I leave?

They're gone. Messages exist only in the browser memory of the people in the conversation during the session. Close the tab and they vanish — permanently and immediately.

Why text chat beats video roulette

Video-based platforms like Chatroulette have a fundamental problem: the camera is a barrier. You need good lighting, a tidy background, a working webcam, and the willingness to be seen by a stranger in the first second. Most people are skipped before they can say hello.

Text removes all of that. On Chattyio, conversations start with words. You can be on your phone in bed, at your desk at work, or anywhere else — no one sees you until you decide to describe yourself. The quality of the conversation matters, not how you look on camera.

Text also eliminates the inappropriate content problem that has plagued video chat platforms for years. Chattyio is clean by design — text only, links blocked, bots filtered out, and every user verified as 18+.
